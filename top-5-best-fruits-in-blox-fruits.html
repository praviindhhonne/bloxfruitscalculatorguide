Top 5 Best Fruits in Blox Fruits

Here’s a list of the top 5 fruits in Blox Fruits and why they’re so powerful:

  1. Flame Fruit: Great for AoE damage and crowd control.
  2. Ice Fruit: Excellent for defense and freezing enemies.
  3. Dark Fruit: High damage output and versatile abilities.
  4. Light Fruit: Fast movement and strong ranged attacks.
  5. Quake Fruit: Devastating area damage and knockback effects.

Choose the fruit that best suits your playstyle and dominate the game!
